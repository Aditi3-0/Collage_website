SOCIOLOGY

About the Subject

Sociology is the scientific study of human social life, relationships and behaviour. Its emergence in the 19th century was a response to the effects of the French, scientific and industrial revolutions. In the 21st century, we are confronted with transformative changes that have resulted from urbanization, globalization, marketization, information explosion, technological revolution and digitalization. Sociology also examines current social problems associated with marriage, education, polity, beliefs, discrimination, casteism, inequality, suicide, gender, sexuality, overpopulation, unemployment, gadget addiction, intolerance, violence, lack of representation, and so on.
